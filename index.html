Run an extra mile easily by building endurance gradually and focusing on consistency. Begin with small steps, increase distance slowly, and maintain a steady pace. Proper hydration, warm-ups, and good form will boost stamina, while mental focus helps overcome challenges, making every extra mile feel more achievable.
